Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

McJohnson Wallet ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website or use the McJohnson Wallet mobile application.

üîí Core Privacy Principle

McJohnson Wallet is non-custodial and privacy-focused.

  • We do NOT have access to your private keys, seed phrase, or wallet funds
  • We do NOT track your wallet balances or transaction history
  • All wallet data is stored locally and securely on your device
  • You have complete control over your cryptocurrency

1. Information We DO NOT Collect (Mobile App)

When you use the McJohnson Wallet mobile application, we DO NOT collect or have access to:

  • Private keys or seed phrases (stored only on your device)
  • Wallet balances or cryptocurrency holdings
  • Transaction history or payment details
  • Personally identifiable information unless voluntarily provided
  • Your location, contacts, or photos (unless required by a Mini App with your permission)

2. Information We DO Collect

2.1 Website Analytics (This Website Only)

When you visit this promotional website (not the mobile app), we automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, links clicked
  • Network Information: Anonymized IP address, general location (city/country)
  • Referral Source: How you found our website

Purpose: To understand website traffic, improve user experience, and optimize content.

2.2 Mobile App - Local Storage Only

The McJohnson Wallet app stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Wallet Credentials: Private keys and seed phrase (encrypted)
  • User Preferences: App settings, theme, custom username/handle
  • Mini App Permissions: Which Mini Apps you've authorized

Important: This data NEVER leaves your device and is NOT transmitted to our servers.

2.3 Contact Information (Voluntary)

If you contact us via email or support channels, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Name (if provided)
  • Message content and attachments

Purpose: To respond to your inquiries and provide support.

2.4 Crash Reports & Diagnostics (Optional)

If you opt-in to send crash reports, we may collect:

  • App version and device model
  • Error logs and stack traces
  • Operating system version

Note: Crash reports do NOT contain wallet data, private keys, or transaction details.

2.5 Blockchain Data (Public by Nature)

  • All LTCO and POL transactions are recorded on the public Polygon blockchain
  • Anyone can view wallet addresses and transactions via blockchain explorers (e.g., PolygonScan)
  • We do NOT link blockchain addresses to personal identities
  • Blockchain data is NOT collected by us‚Äîit's inherent to blockchain technology

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Website Analytics: Improve website design, content, and performance
  • Customer Support: Respond to inquiries and troubleshoot issues
  • App Improvement: Fix bugs and enhance user experience (via crash reports)
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Compliance: Meet legal and regulatory obligations

We do NOT use your information for:

  • Selling or renting to third parties
  • Targeted advertising
  • Tracking individual user behavior across devices

5. Mini-Applications & Third-Party Services

When you use Mini Apps within McJohnson Wallet:

  • Each Mini App is developed by independent third-party developers
  • Mini Apps have their own privacy policies
  • You must explicitly grant permissions for each app (wallet access, camera, etc.)
  • We are NOT responsible for third-party Mini App data practices
  • Review each app's permissions and privacy policy before use
  • You can revoke permissions at any time in app settings

6. Data Storage & Security

6.1 Local Device Storage (Mobile App)

Your sensitive wallet data is stored locally on your device:

  • Private keys: Encrypted using AES-256 encryption
  • Seed phrase: Stored only on your device (NEVER on our servers)
  • Biometric protection: Optional fingerprint/Face ID authentication

We CANNOT access or recover this data.

6.2 Website Data Storage

Website analytics data is stored securely by our analytics provider with industry-standard encryption.

6.3 Security Measures

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all website communications
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to any collected data
  • No storage of private keys or wallet credentials on our servers

6.4 No Absolute Security

While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. You are responsible for:

  • Securing your device with a strong password/PIN
  • Safely storing your seed phrase offline
  • Never sharing your private keys

7. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

This website (not the mobile app) uses cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Track website usage (anonymized)
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit website functionality.

The mobile app does NOT use cookies or tracking technologies.
